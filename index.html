About Us

We pay you for your non-used devices

Revive is a passionate team working to reduce electronic waste by collecting unused or broken devices from homes, refurbishing what can be saved, and recycling the rest safely. Based in Egypt and aiming for global impact, we use modern tools to manage our operations, ensure transparency, and raise awareness about sustainable tech practices through education and partnerships.
